User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to our support team at support@example.com with the subject "Delete My Data".
  2. In the email, please include your username and the email address associated with your account.
  3. Our support team will process your request within 7 days and confirm via email once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team.
